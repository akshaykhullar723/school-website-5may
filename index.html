VISION AND MISSION

Mission statement

Western Province Cricket is to provide access and opportunity for all communities to participate in cricket and lead in transforming our cricket fraternity. We promise to be in constant pursuit of excellence and professionalism to better engage with all stakeholders. We strive to become world leaders on and off the field, and our stadium to be the best cricket ground in the world.

Vision statement

To pursue the passion of “The Champions Within”.

Values statement

  • Honesty and integrity: Tell the truth and act consistently on a set of ethical principles.
  • Mutual respect and fairness: Value our people (employees, players, clubs) and encourage their development and reward their performance.
  • Transformation: Strive to afford real quality of playing opportunity to PDI communities and in particular black African players. Provide access to all women in cricket and opportunity to disabled cricket players.
  • Professionalism and diligence: To diligently provide a professional service.
